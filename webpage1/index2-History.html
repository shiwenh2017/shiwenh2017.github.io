History

I currently study philosophy and am in my very last semester at NYU.

Interestingly enough, philosophy is something that I probably would never have imagined myself being interested in, not to mention majoring in it in college. When I was in elementary school I wanted to be a scientist. When I was in middle school, I wanted to be a video game designer. When I was in high school, I excelled in math and physics, but I wanted to be in business, to be a stock investor. And that was what I went into NYU thinking I’d dom, which is why I went to Stern, NYU’s supposedly famous business school. However, 2 years later into NYU, I had changed my major three times, and had finally settled on philosophy.
